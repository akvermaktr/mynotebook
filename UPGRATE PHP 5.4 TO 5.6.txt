Quick Walk through on how to upgrade PHP:

 

Verify current version:

php -v

 

Ensure you have the EPEL and webstatic yum reposititories

rpm -Uvh https://mirror.webtatic.com/yum/el7/epel-release.rpm

rpm -Uvh https://mirror.webtatic.com/yum/el7/webtatic-release.rpm

 

Remove the php-common package which conflicts on installation of the new package:

yum remove php-common -y

 

Reinstall PHP, version 5.6 ofcourse

yum install -y php56w php56w-opcache php56w-xml php56w-mcrypt php56w-gd php56w-devel php56w-mysql php56w-intl php56w-mbstring

 

Restart HTTPD (apache)

sudo systemctl restart httpd
